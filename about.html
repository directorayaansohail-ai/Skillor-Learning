Mission & Vision

Why we built Skillor.

The digital economy is changing at the speed of AI. We're here to make sure Kerala's students aren't left behind.

Our Mission

Empower every student with AI-ready skills

To provide the most practical, up-to-date AI + digital marketing education in Kerala — giving every student the tools, skills, and confidence to earn and grow in the new digital economy. No fluff, no theory-only classes. Just real skills that work in the real world.

Our Vision

Become India's leading AI-first digital academy

To build a generation of AI-powered digital entrepreneurs, marketers, content creators, and freelancers who can compete globally. We envision Skillor as the go-to name for anyone in India who wants to master AI + digital skills and build a future-proof career or business.

Our Promise

Skills that translate directly to income

Every module, every session, every project is designed with one question in mind: will this make our students more employable, more freelanceable, and more competitive? If it doesn't pass that test, it doesn't make it into the curriculum.

Our Approach

95% practical. 100% mentor-led. Always live.

We don't believe in pre-recorded passive learning. Skillor is built around live sessions, real feedback, real projects, and a mentor who has actually done what he teaches. This is the difference between a certificate and a skill.

Brand Story

How Skillor came to be.

2019
Ayaan starts his digital journey
Begins freelancing, content creation, and running digital campaigns for local Kerala businesses from scratch.
2021
First international brand collaboration
Ayaan collaborates with international brands on AI-powered campaigns, building a reputation for results-first digital marketing.
2022
AI integration begins
Builds and manages multiple AI-integrated businesses, pioneers AI-driven content creation and campaign automation in Kerala.
2023
The idea for Skillor is born
Seeing students struggle with outdated courses, Ayaan decides to create the academy he wished had existed when he started.
2024–25
Skillor Learning launches
Kerala's first AI-integrated digital marketing academy launches with its flagship 3-month live course. First batch delivers real results.

"I spent years figuring out AI + digital marketing the hard way — expensive mistakes, outdated tutorials, and wasted time. Skillor exists so students in Kerala don't have to go through that."

Ayaan Sohail is a digital entrepreneur, content creator, and theatre advertisement director with 5+ years of real-world experience. He's worked with international brands, run AI-powered campaigns, and built multiple businesses from the ground up — all before founding Skillor.

The name Skillor is built on one idea: skills that matter, taught by someone who lives them. Every module in the curriculum is something Ayaan has personally used to build a real business or campaign. Nothing is theoretical. Everything is battle-tested.

Today, Skillor Learning is the only academy in Kerala that teaches AI tools, digital marketing, video editing, e-commerce, and freelancing in a single live, mentor-led program — with a focus on portfolio-building, placements, and real income.
